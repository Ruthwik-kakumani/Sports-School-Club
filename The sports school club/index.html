About Us

The Sports School is a premier multisport training centre in Bangalore, India offering expert coaching and state-of-the-art facilities. It serves as a comprehensive training destination for both aspiring and elite athletes. After achieving remarkable success in Bangalore, The Sports School expects the same from TSS Cricket Academy in the USA.

As always, our USP lies in our international coaches, hailing from cricket powerhouses like India, Australia, and New Zealand. These seasoned professionals bring unparalleled expertise and a wealth of experience to nurture budding talent.
At TSS Cricket Academy, we believe in starting young. Our focus on grassroots training ensures that we identify promising players at the right age, providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel on a global stage. Our learner-students will have the incredible opportunity to compete in tournaments in India, gaining invaluable exposure and experience.

We are bringing our experience, passion and mastery of cricket from India to the USA. Our attention to detail and technical savvy in the sport sets us apart, making us one of the best cricket academies in the world. Join us at TSS Cricket Academy and be part of a journey that transforms young enthusiasts into world-class cricketers.
